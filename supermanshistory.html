Supermans History

Superman is a fictional superhero. He was the the first dc superhero. The character was created by writer Jerry Siegel. The character was drawn by Joe Shuster. Superman was born on the planet Krypton. Who named him Clark Kent. Clark developed various superhuman abilities. Such as incredible strength and impervious skin. His foster parents advised him to use his abilities for the benefit of humanity. And he decided to fight crime as a vigilante. He was the best-selling superhero character in American comic books up until the 1980s. Supermans weakness is kroptonite. Superman can fly at great speeds. Superman can project and perceive X-rays via his eyes. Superman has freeze breath. Seeing through objects. Kirk Alyn was the first actor of superman. Superman's secret identity is Clark kent. His full name is Clark Joseph Kent. He is a reporter for the Daily Planet.


Supermans Past

Given the name Kal-El at birth. As a baby, his parents sent him to Earth in a small spaceship. Moments before Krypton was destroyed in a natural cataclysm. His ship landed in the American countryside, near the fictional town of Smallville. He was found and adopted by farmers Jonathan and Martha Kent. To protect his privacy, he changes into a colorful costume and uses the alias "Superman" when fighting crime. Clark Kent resides in the fictional American city of Metropolis. Where he works as a journalist for the Daily Planet. Daily Planet photographer Jimmy Olsen and editor-in-chief Perry White. His arch-enemy being supervillain Lex Luthor.
